Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The Copy Machine ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you purchase and use The Copy Machine Claude Skill.

What We Collect

Purchase Information

When you purchase The Copy Machine, our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy) collects:

  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Lemon Squeezy — we never see your full card details)
  • Name (if provided)
  • Country (for tax purposes)

What We Don't Collect

The Copy Machine is a Claude Skill — a set of instructions that runs inside Anthropic's Claude interface. We have no access to:

  • Your Claude conversations
  • The copy you generate or rewrite
  • Your product information or business details
  • Any content you input into Claude

The skill runs entirely within Claude's environment. Your data stays between you and Anthropic.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to:

  • Deliver your purchase (the skill files and installation instructions)
  • Send important product updates (rare — only if something breaks or significantly improves)
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We don't send marketing emails. We don't sell or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. They handle your payment securely and are PCI-DSS compliant. We receive confirmation of your purchase but never your full payment details. Review Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Data Storage and Security

Purchase records are stored securely through Lemon Squeezy's infrastructure. We retain your email for order fulfillment and support purposes. We don't store data beyond what's necessary to provide you with your purchase and respond to support requests.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request your data: Ask us what information we have about you
  • Request deletion: Ask us to delete your information (note: we may need to retain purchase records for accounting/tax purposes)
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of any product update emails (link in every email)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email on our Contact page.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. Our payment processor may use cookies during checkout. We don't use tracking or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Lemon Squeezy: Payment processing and order management
  • Anthropic (Claude): The AI platform where the skill runs — governed by Anthropic's privacy policy

Each service has its own privacy policy. When you use Claude, Anthropic's policies govern how your conversations and data are handled.

Children's Privacy

The Copy Machine is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date and, if warranted, notify customers by email. Continued use of the product after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us through our Contact page.

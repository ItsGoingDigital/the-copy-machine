Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The Short Version

All sales of The Copy Machine are final. We do not offer refunds.

Why No Refunds?

The Copy Machine is a digital product — a Claude Skill consisting of text-based instruction files. Once you receive the files, you have immediate, permanent access to the complete product. There's no way to "return" digital knowledge.

Unlike software subscriptions, this is a one-time purchase. You're not paying for ongoing access that can be revoked — you're buying a set of copywriting frameworks, tone guides, and instructions that you keep forever.

What You Get Before Purchase

We want you to buy with confidence. Before purchasing, you have access to:

  • Full product description on our homepage explaining exactly what The Copy Machine does
  • Before/after examples showing the difference the skill makes
  • Clear explanation of how the skill works (framework + tone + channel)
  • This refund policy — you know what you're agreeing to

Exceptions

We may consider exceptions in rare cases:

  • Duplicate purchase: If you accidentally bought twice, contact us and we'll refund the duplicate
  • Technical delivery failure: If our system fails to deliver the product and we can't resolve it, we'll refund you

These situations require proof (order confirmations, screenshots of errors, etc.). Contact us through our Contact page with details.

Before You Buy

Make sure The Copy Machine is right for you:

  • You need Claude access. This is a Claude Skill — it runs inside Claude. You need an active Claude account (Claude.ai or API access).
  • It's a writing tool, not magic. The skill applies copywriting frameworks to your inputs. You still need to provide product context. Results depend on what you put in.
  • It's for copy, not code. This skill writes landing pages, emails, ads, and sales pages. It doesn't write software.

Questions?

Not sure if The Copy Machine is right for your use case? Contact us before purchasing. We'd rather answer questions upfront than have you buy something that doesn't fit your needs.
